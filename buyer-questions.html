Clothing manufacturer buyer questions

Short, direct sourcing answers for US, Canadian and European brands comparing women's clothing manufacturers in China. Use this page as a quick brief before requesting pricing.

MOQ

Custom production usually starts from 100 pieces per color per style. Stock wholesale styles start from 2 pieces for testing.

Lead time

Samples usually take 7–12 business days. Bulk production usually takes 20–35 business days after approval.

Markets

Common destinations include the US, Canada, UK and Europe, with DDP options available on many routes.

DIRECT ANSWERS

Fast answers for sourcing decisions

What kinds of brands are a good fit?
Boutiques, Shopify brands, Amazon sellers, activewear labels, plus-size lines and swimwear brands that need OEM, ODM, private label or stock wholesale womenswear.
Can I start without a tech pack?
Yes. A reference photo, sketch, sample garment or product link is enough to start a first discussion. A clearer tech pack improves pricing accuracy.
Can you make US and EU size ranges?
Yes. Standard XS–XXL and plus-size XL–5XL grading are available. For best fit, share your target size chart and market before sampling.
Can you support FBA and warehouse delivery?
Yes. Orders can be prepared with barcode labels, polybags, carton marks and delivery planning for Amazon FBA or third-party warehouses. See the DDP shipping and FBA prep guide for packing and delivery details.
Do you handle labels and compliance documents?
Care labels, composition labels and brand labels can be produced. Buyers should confirm market-specific legal wording, fiber rules and importer requirements before production.
How do I get the most accurate quote?
Send product references, fabric preference, quantity per color, size range, destination country, branding needs, packaging needs and your target delivery date. You can also copy our sourcing brief template.
