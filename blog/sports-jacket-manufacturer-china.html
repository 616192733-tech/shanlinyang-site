Sports Jacket Manufacturer in China: Custom Zip-Up & Track Jackets for Brands

PUBLISHED 2026-07-06 · SHANLINYANG APPAREL FACTORY TEAM · ~13 MIN READ

Outerwear is a critical component of any activewear brand's collection. A well-designed sports jacket not only provides protection from the elements but also serves as a versatile piece for "to and from" the gym. From lightweight windbreakers to supportive zip-up track jackets, the manufacturing of sports outerwear requires a higher level of technical skill compared to basic tees. For brands looking to scale, finding an experienced sports jacket manufacturer in China is the key to accessing advanced technical fabrics and complex garment construction at a competitive price.

China's manufacturing ecosystem for technical outerwear is the most sophisticated in the world. Whether you need seam-sealed waterproof jackets, breathable running windbreakers, or stylish athleisure zip-ups, Chinese factories offer unparalleled expertise. In this guide, we will explore the essential technical features of sports jackets, the best fabrics for performance outerwear, and how to work with a manufacturer to create a successful collection. At Shanlinyang Apparel, we specialize in helping brands create functional, high-quality sports jackets with low MOQs.

1. Popular Styles of Sports Jackets We Manufacture

Diversity in your outerwear line allows you to serve different customer needs throughout the seasons. Here are the core styles we specialize in:

Performance Zip-Up Track Jackets

These are typically slim-fit jackets made from high-stretch fabrics like Nylon/Spandex or Scuba. They are designed for warmth during warm-ups or cool-downs. Key features include high neck collars, thumbholes at the cuffs, and secure zip pockets. We focus on ensuring the zippers are smooth and the fit is tailored to provide a flattering, athletic silhouette.

Lightweight Running Windbreakers

Running jackets must be incredibly light and breathable. We use technical polyester or nylon fabrics with wind-resistant properties. These jackets often feature laser-cut ventilation under the arms or on the back to prevent overheating. They are also designed to be "packable," allowing the runner to easily store the jacket if the weather changes.

Fleece-Lined Thermal Jackets

For colder climates, we manufacture jackets with a brushed fleece interior. These provide excellent thermal insulation while maintaining moisture-wicking properties. These are ideal for outdoor hiking, winter running, or as a warm layer for yoga practitioners traveling to the studio in winter.

Hooded Training Jackets

A versatile training jacket with a hood is a staple for both men's and women's activewear. We can customize hoods with adjustable drawstrings, high-profile "scuba" hoods for wind protection, and specialized linings for extra comfort.

2. Performance Fabrics for Technical Outerwear

The fabric is the most important factor in a jacket's functionality. We source a wide range of technical textiles designed for specific activities.

Durable Water Repellent (DWR) Fabrics

For jackets intended for outdoor use, we apply a DWR finish to the outer shell. This causes water to bead up and roll off the surface, keeping the wearer dry in light rain without compromising breathability. We offer eco-friendly C0 and C6 DWR treatments to meet global sustainability standards.

Breathable Mesh Linings

Outerwear can often trap heat. To combat this, we use high-quality mesh linings that improve airflow between the wearer's body and the jacket shell. This is especially important for high-intensity activities like running or cycling.

4-Way Stretch Performance Fabrics

Many of our zip-up jackets use "Interlock" or "Scuba" fabrics that offer significant 4-way stretch. This allows the athlete to move freely without the jacket feeling restrictive. These fabrics also offer excellent shape retention, ensuring the jacket doesn't become "baggy" over time.

Reflective Materials for Safety

For running and cycling jackets, safety is paramount. We can incorporate reflective piping, reflective logos, or even full reflective fabric panels that become highly visible when hit by car headlights. This is a crucial feature for any brand targeting outdoor athletes.

3. Technical Features and Hardware Selection

The "hardware" of a jacket—the zippers, toggles, and drawstrings—can make or break the user experience. We source only high-quality components for our outerwear.

Waterproof and Heat-Sealed Zippers

For premium performance jackets, we use heat-sealed zippers that prevent water from leaking through the teeth. We also offer "auto-lock" sliders that stay in place even during vigorous movement, preventing the jacket from accidentally unzipping.

Functional Details: Thumbholes and Pockets

Functional details add significant value. Thumbholes keep sleeves in place and provide extra warmth for the hands. We also specialize in "invisible" zip pockets and internal "media pockets" with cord ports for headphones—features that modern gym-goers love.

Chin Guards and Zipper Garages

To prevent the zipper from scratching the wearer's chin, we include "zipper garages" at the top of the collar. This small detail is a hallmark of high-end activewear manufacturing and shows your customers that you care about their comfort.

4. The Manufacturing and Quality Control Process

Jackets are complex garments with many components. Our QC process is designed to ensure every piece meets your brand's standards.

Seam Strength and Durability Testing

Jackets undergo significant stress at the armholes and shoulders. We perform seam-strength tests to ensure the stitching can withstand the tension of active movement. We use high-quality bonded threads that are resistant to friction and moisture.

Water-Resistance and Breathability Testing

For technical outerwear, we perform lab tests to verify the fabric's water-resistance (measured in mm) and breathability (measured in g/m²/24h). This allows you to accurately market your product's performance capabilities to your customers.

Hardware Functional Check

Our QC team checks every single zipper and drawstring on the production line. We ensure that the zippers move smoothly and that the heat-sealing is perfectly aligned. A faulty zipper is the most common reason for jacket returns, so we take this stage very seriously.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the minimum order for custom sports jackets?
For custom OEM jackets with your design, our MOQ is 100 pieces per color per style. We also offer wholesale options starting from 2 pieces.

Q2: Can you manufacture jackets with waterproof zippers?
Yes. We can source technical hardware including heat-sealed waterproof zippers and auto-lock sliders for performance gear.

Q3: What fabrics are best for windbreakers?
Lightweight Nylon or Polyester with a DWR (Durable Water Repellent) finish is ideal. These fabrics are wind-resistant yet breathable.

Yes, thumbholes are a common functional feature we include in many of our activewear jacket designs.
