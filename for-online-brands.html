FAQ — ONLINE BRANDS

FAQ

Can you ship directly to Amazon FBA warehouses?
Yes. Delivery can be planned for Amazon FBA, 3PL warehouses or buyer addresses in the US and Europe when label and carton rules are confirmed before packing.
Do you protect my private-label designs?
Customer-exclusive designs, prints and trims are not offered to other buyers. NDA workflows are available on request.
What should I send for a quote?
Send product photos or tech packs, target quantity, size/color breakdown, logo and packaging needs, destination country and target launch date.
