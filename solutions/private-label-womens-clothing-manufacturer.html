Private label FAQ

Questions buyers ask before private-label production

Can I private label existing stock styles?

Yes. This is often the fastest route for boutiques and new brands. You can test a style first, then add your logo, woven labels, hangtags, and packaging when you are ready for a branded run.

Can you make private-label clothing from my tech pack?

Yes. If you already have a tech pack, Shanlinyang can review construction, fabric, sizing, branding, and quantity details before sampling and production.

Can I start private label without a tech pack?

Yes. You can start with photos, sketches, product links, or a physical sample. The factory may ask follow-up questions before quoting accurately.

What should I send for a quote?

Send product reference, quantity, size range, fabric direction, logo/label needs, packaging, destination, and deadline. If unsure, use the quote checklist page first.
