LOW-RISK LAUNCH PATH

How startup production works

  1. Test styles first

    Use available stock styles from 2 pieces to check fabric, fit, photos and customer response.

  2. Confirm your brand details

    Choose labels, hangtags, logo placement, packaging and the size range your audience needs.

  3. Approve a sample

    Review fit, sewing, logo and fabric before committing to bulk production.

  4. Scale winners

    Move validated styles into private-label or custom production from 100 pcs/color/style.
