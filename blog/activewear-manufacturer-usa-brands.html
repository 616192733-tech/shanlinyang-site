Activewear Manufacturer for US Brands: China Sourcing Without the Headaches

PUBLISHED 2026-06-23 · SHANLINYANG APPAREL FACTORY TEAM · ~13 MIN READ

The United States is the world's largest market for activewear and athleisure, but it is also one of the most demanding. US consumers expect high-performance fabrics, inclusive sizing, and impeccable brand ethics. For a US-based brand, the challenge is finding an activewear manufacturer who can deliver this quality from China while navigating the complexities of international trade, labeling regulations, and logistics. You need a partner who doesn't just make clothes, but understands the specific requirements of the American market.

At Shanlinyang Apparel, we have dedicated years to serving the US market. From our Xiamen facility, we provide a seamless "factory-to-door" service that removes the headaches typically associated with overseas sourcing. In this guide, we'll cover the essential details every US brand owner needs to know.

1. Mastering US Sizing and Fit

One of the biggest pitfalls for US brands sourcing from China is the "size gap." Chinese standard sizing is significantly smaller than US standard sizing. As an experienced activewear manufacturer for USA brands, we eliminate this risk:

Master US Charts: We have developed master size charts specifically for the American body type, ensuring that your "Medium" actually fits like a US Medium. We focus on torso length, bust accommodation, and thigh width—areas where Chinese patterns often fall short.

Inclusive Sizing: Diversity is a core value in the US market. We offer specialized pattern grading for plus-size activewear up to 5XL. We ensure that the compression and support are maintained across the entire size range, not just the smaller sizes.

2. Compliance and Labeling for the US Market

The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) has strict rules for apparel labeling in the US. Failure to comply can lead to your goods being seized at customs. A professional activewear manufacturer ensures your labels meet all requirements:

  • Fiber Content: Must list the percentage of each fiber (e.g., 80% Nylon, 20% Spandex).
  • Country of Origin: "Made in China" must be clearly visible.
  • Manufacturer Identity: This can be your brand name or your Registered Identification Number (RN number).
  • Care Instructions: Must be permanent and follow standard US care symbols and terminology.

We provide "tagless" heat-transfer labels that meet all these requirements while providing a comfortable, chafe-free experience for the wearer.

3. Logistics: From Xiamen to your US Door

Shipping from China to the US can be daunting, especially with fluctuating tariffs and customs regulations. We offer several "headache-free" logistics options:

DDP (Delivered Duty Paid): This is our most popular service for US brands. We handle the export from China, the ocean or air freight, the US customs clearance, and the payment of all duties and taxes. Your goods are delivered directly to your warehouse or 3PL, and you only receive one all-inclusive invoice from us.

Amazon FBA Prep: If you sell on Amazon, we are experts in FBA requirements. We handle individual polybagging, FNSKU labeling, and Master Carton labeling. We can ship directly to Amazon's fulfillment centers, saving you the time and cost of double-handling.

4. Technical Excellence: Performance Fabrics for the US Consumer

American fitness enthusiasts are technical. They know their fabrics. As a leading activewear manufacturer, we offer the high-spec materials your customers demand:

  • Squat-Proof Opacity: We use high-density Interlock knits (280-320 GSM) to ensure your leggings are never see-through.
  • Buttery Soft Finishes: We offer "brushed" and "peached" nylons that replicate the high-end feel of brands like Lululemon.
  • Sustainability: The US market is leading the charge for eco-friendly apparel. We source GRS-certified recycled polyesters and organic cottons for conscious brands.

5. Quality Control: The Shanlinyang Standard

To succeed in the US, your quality must be consistent. Our Xiamen facility implements a multi-stage QC process for all our activewear and fitness apparel:

  1. Fabric Lab Testing: We check for colorfastness, shrinkage (important for the high-heat dryers common in US homes), and pilling.
  2. Symmetry and Construction: We ensure that logos are perfectly straight and that seams are durable and flat.
  3. Final Measurement Audit: Every batch is measured against the approved US size chart before it leaves the factory.

FAQ: Sourcing for the US Market

What is an RN number, and do I need one?

An RN (Registered Identification Number) is a number issued by the FTC to US-based businesses. It's not mandatory (you can use your brand name instead), but it is common for established US labels.

How do you handle the Section 301 tariffs on Chinese apparel?

Tariffs are included in our DDP shipping quotes. We work with our logistics partners to find the most cost-effective shipping routes to minimize the impact on your margins.

Can you replicate a US brand's specific fit?

Yes. If you send us a physical sample of a garment you love, our pattern makers can create a 'fit-replica' tech pack for your production.

What if I want to use my own shipping forwarder?

No problem. We can provide FOB (Free on Board) terms and work with your preferred logistics provider to handle the export from Xiamen.

How do I ensure my designs are protected?

We work with many international brands and respect intellectual property. We are happy to sign an NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) before you share your detailed designs.
