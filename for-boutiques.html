WHO WE SERVE — FOR BOUTIQUE OWNERS

The wholesale clothing vendor built for boutique buying cycles

Boutiques don't buy like big retail — you test small, restock fast, and drop what doesn't sell. Our wholesale program is built around exactly that rhythm.

Buy the way a boutique sells

Open-pack ordering from 2 pieces per style, mixed sizes, colors and styles in one carton. Test five dresses with a $100 order instead of betting on one style at 100 units. All garments graded to US size charts so your customers get the fit they expect.

Restock at boutique speed

In-stock styles ship in 1–5 days, air express to the US in 5–8 days. When a style sells through on Friday, replenishment can be on your shelf within two weeks — including busy season.

Graduate winners into your own label

Once a style proves itself, move it into custom production from 100 pcs per color: your woven labels, hangtags and packaging at factory pricing. Most of our long-term boutique partners run this exact stock-test-then-brand playbook.

Line sheets done for you

Ask for our line sheet and we'll send categorized photo catalogs with FOB tiers — dresses, sets, curve, swim — updated as the 961+ style catalog rotates each season.

FAQ — FOR BOUTIQUE OWNERS

FAQ

Is there a minimum order amount?
No minimum order value — stock styles start at 2 pieces per style and you can mix freely across the catalog.
Do you handle US customs?
Yes, we can quote DDP (duties paid, door-to-door) so the price you approve is the landed price.
Can I get fabric details before ordering?
Yes — every quote includes fabric composition and weight, and we can ship swatch cards before bulk stock orders.
