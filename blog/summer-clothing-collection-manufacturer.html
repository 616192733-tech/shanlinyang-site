Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best breathable fabrics for summer?

Linen, organic cotton, and bamboo are excellent for keeping cool in hot weather.

How long does it take to produce a summer swimwear line?

Swimwear production typically takes 45-60 days, excluding fabric sourcing and sampling.

Can I use recycled materials for my summer collection?

Yes, many Chinese manufacturers offer recycled polyester and nylon for sustainable summer wear.

What are the key trends for Summer 2026?

Bright citrus colors, crochet textures, and oversized linen sets are expected to dominate.

How do I ensure UV protection in my summer fabrics?

Look for manufacturers that provide UPF-rated fabrics, especially for active and beachwear.
