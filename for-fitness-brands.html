FAQ — FITNESS & ATHLEISURE LABELS

FAQ

Which fabrics do you recommend for leggings?
Nylon-spandex is common for premium buttery activewear; poly-spandex is useful for custom prints and more price-sensitive programs. The best choice depends on compression, opacity, handfeel and target price.
Can you do all-over custom prints?
Yes. Sublimation and printed activewear programs are available, with MOQ depending on base fabric, print method and color requirements.
How fast can a 3-style capsule launch?
A typical path is quote and fabric alignment first, samples in 7–12 business days, revision if needed, then bulk production after sample approval.
