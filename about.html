WHY BUYERS TRUST US

Factory facts buyers usually ask for first

Export-focused womenswear production

We produce two-piece sets, activewear, dresses, plus-size XL–5XL, swimwear and tracksuits for boutiques, DTC stores, Amazon sellers and wholesale buyers.

Flexible starting path

Buyers can start with stock-style tests, samples or custom private-label runs from 100 pcs per color per style.

QC before shipment

Incoming fabric checks, inline sewing checks and final inspection help catch issues before goods leave the factory.

Remote factory verification

Buyers can request video factory tours, sample QC reports and third-party inspections before or during production.
