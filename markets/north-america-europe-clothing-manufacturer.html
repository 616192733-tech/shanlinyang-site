Quote preparation

What to send for a faster, more useful reply

Product reference

Photos, links, sketches, tech packs, existing samples, or competitor references.

Quantity and size range

Estimated pieces by style, color, and size. Include plus-size needs if relevant.

Fabric and trims

Stretch, compression, opacity, lining, buttons, zippers, elastic, print, embroidery, or special wash requirements.

Branding and packaging

Logo method, woven labels, care labels, hangtags, barcode stickers, polybags, carton marks, and branded packaging.

Destination and delivery route

Country, warehouse, Amazon FBA, 3PL, DDP preference, sea or air timing, and launch deadline.

Quality expectations

Measurement tolerance, sample approval, inspection photos, fabric confirmation, packing checks, and pre-shipment review.

Questions North American and European buyers ask

Can Shanlinyang manufacture clothing for US and European brands?

Yes. Shanlinyang works with buyers in the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, France, and wider European markets. Typical projects include private-label womenswear, activewear, dresses, swimwear, boutique stock tests, and custom OEM/ODM development.

What is the safest first order for a new overseas clothing brand?

The safest route is usually a small stock test or a focused custom run with clear sample approval, confirmed fabric, defined size range, and packaging requirements. Avoid launching too many styles before fit, demand, and restock timing are proven.

Does Shanlinyang help with Amazon FBA and 3PL delivery?

Yes. Buyers can discuss barcode labels, polybags, carton marks, carton packing, DDP delivery, Amazon FBA preparation, and 3PL warehouse delivery before production and packing.

How can a buyer get a more accurate quote?

Send product references, estimated quantity, size range, fabric direction, destination country, logo and label needs, packaging details, delivery deadline, and any marketplace or warehouse requirements.
