COMPANY — XIAMEN SHAN LIN YANG TRADING CO., LTD.

A women's fashion factory in Xiamen, built for export brands

Founded in 2018 and backed by a production team with over 20 years in sportswear and fashion manufacturing, Xiamen Shan Lin Yang Trading Co., Ltd. runs a 3,500 m² facility in Xiamen, Fujian — one of China's main garment export hubs, 20 minutes from Xiamen port.

What we make

Women's fashion and embroidered apparel: two-piece and three-piece sets, tracksuits, activewear and yoga wear, dresses, plus size XL–5XL and swimwear. Our fabric strength is in light, drape-heavy materials — silk, silk-touch polyester, chiffon, lace — alongside athleisure knits: waffle, velour, french terry and spandex blends.

Who we work with

Boutique owners, Shopify and Amazon clothing brands, and wholesalers across the United States, Europe, Canada and Australia, plus customers in Japan, Korea and the Middle East. Long-term partners typically start with a 2-piece stock test order, then move winning styles into 100-piece custom runs.

Quality control

Every order passes three checkpoints: incoming fabric inspection (color, weight, defects), inline sewing QC on the production floor, and a final AQL inspection with photographed reports before packing. Third-party inspection (SGS, TÜV, or your own agent) is welcome at any stage.

Visit us

Buyer visits are welcome — Xiamen has direct flights from most Asian hubs. Can't travel? We run live video factory tours over WhatsApp or Zoom before your first order. Book a tour through the inquiry form.

Shanlinyang Apparel — Women's Clothing Manufacturer in Xiamen, China

Founded in 2018, Shanlinyang Apparel (厦门衫琳漾贸易有限公司) is a professional OEM/ODM women's clothing manufacturer based in Xiamen, Fujian Province, China. We operate a 3,500 m² production facility with a dedicated team of 50–200 skilled workers, specializing in private label activewear, two-piece sets, dresses, plus-size clothing (XL–5XL), swimwear, and tracksuits for boutiques and DTC brands worldwide.

Our manufacturing capabilities cover the full garment production cycle: fabric sourcing, pattern making, sample development, bulk production, quality inspection, and export packing. We work with brands across the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Europe, and 30+ countries, offering flexible MOQ starting from just 2 pieces for stock styles and 100 pieces for custom private label orders.
