TRUST — PRODUCTION CASES

How buyers actually work with us

Three recent production stories, anonymized to protect client exclusivity, showing the paths most partners take: stock-test then brand, capsule launch, and fit rescue.

DRESSES · BOUTIQUE CHAIN · US SOUTH

From a 50-piece stock test to a branded 600-piece program

A three-store boutique group ordered 50 pieces of our halter backless dress (style LYSKMEQ-116) across mixed sizes as a stock test. Sell-through in the first month justified scale: we developed two exclusive colorways with their woven labels and hangtags, 600 pieces total, delivered DDP to their Texas distribution point.

ACTIVEWEAR · SHOPIFY STARTUP · UK

A 3-style capsule from tech pack to FBA in 9 weeks

A first-time athleisure founder came with reference photos, not tech packs. Our pattern room developed a bra top + legging set, a crop hoodie and bike shorts; samples shipped in 10 days with opacity and stretch reports. Bulk ran 100 pcs per color with silicone waistband logos, barcoded poly bags and Amazon-compliant cartons shipped DDP to a UK FBA warehouse.

PLUS SIZE · CURVE LABEL · EU

Cutting return rates with real curve blocks

A European curve brand was producing XL–4XL on scaled-up straight-size patterns and seeing fit complaints concentrate at 3XL+. We re-graded their three core styles on our dedicated plus-size blocks — adjusted rise, bust dart and arm openings — fit-tested on 2XL forms before bulk. The first restock shipped in sizes XL–5XL.
