BUYER QUESTIONS

Before you ask — asked & answered

What is the MOQ for custom women's clothing?
Custom OEM/ODM production starts at 100 pieces per color per style. In-stock styles ship from just 2 pieces so you can test before committing.
Can you add my own logo and labels?
Yes — logo printing or embroidery, woven neck labels, custom hangtags, care labels and branded poly bags, on our styles or yours.
How long do sampling and production take?
Samples in 7–12 days; bulk in 20–35 days; air express to the US/EU adds 5–8 days.
Can I get a video factory tour before ordering?
Yes. We run live WhatsApp or Zoom factory tours for overseas buyers before their first order, and send photo/video updates during production.
How do I get a quotation?
Use the inquiry form or message us on WhatsApp with style numbers or reference photos — quotes within 24 hours on business days.
