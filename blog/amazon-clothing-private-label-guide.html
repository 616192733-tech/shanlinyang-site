Amazon Private Label Clothing: Complete Guide to Sourcing & Launching on FBA

PUBLISHED 2026-06-26 · SHANLINYANG APPAREL FACTORY TEAM · ~8 MIN READ

Amazon FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon) remains one of the most powerful platforms for launching a private label clothing brand. However, the apparel category is highly competitive and has unique challenges such as high return rates and complex sizing requirements. This guide will walk you through the essential steps of sourcing quality garments from China and successfully launching your brand on Amazon.

Choosing the Right Product Niche for Amazon FBA

Success on Amazon begins with product selection. In the clothing category, you want to find a balance between high demand and manageable competition. Instead of broad terms like "women's dresses," look for specific niches like "seamless yoga sets" or "velvet lounge wear." Use tools like Jungle Scout or Helium 10 to analyze search volume and competitor reviews. Pay close attention to "fit" complaints in competitor reviews; if you can solve a common sizing issue with your manufacturer, you have a significant competitive advantage.

Finding and Vetting a China Clothing Manufacturer

Once you have a niche, you need a reliable partner in China. For Amazon private label, you need a manufacturer that understands Western sizing standards and quality expectations. Platforms like Alibaba are a good starting point, but you must vet potential suppliers thoroughly. Ask for their past export experience to the US or Europe. At Shanlinyang Apparel, we specialize in helping Amazon sellers by providing low MOQs (starting at 100 pieces for custom designs) and high-quality production that minimizes returns. Always order samples before committing to a bulk order to verify fabric quality, stitching, and fit.

Brand Registry and Amazon Listing Optimization

To truly build a brand on Amazon, you must enroll in Amazon Brand Registry. This requires a registered trademark but unlocks essential features like A+ Content (formerly EBC) and a dedicated Brand Store. When creating your listing, focus on high-quality photography—Amazon is a visual platform. Your main image must be on a pure white background. For clothing, lifestyle images showing the fit on different body types can significantly improve conversion rates. Use keywords like "Amazon private label clothing guide" naturally in your title and bullet points to improve SEO ranking.

Handling FBA Logistics and Packaging Requirements

Shipping clothing to Amazon FBA centers requires strict adherence to their packaging guidelines. Garments must be individually bagged in clear polybags. If the bag opening is 5 inches or larger, it must have a suffocation warning. Each unit needs an FNSKU label, which is unique to your Amazon seller account. Most experienced Chinese manufacturers are familiar with these requirements and can apply the labels for you during the production process. For shipping, many sellers use DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) services from freight forwarders, which covers all customs duties and delivers directly to the FBA warehouse.

Managing Returns and Scaling Your Apparel Brand

Returns are an inevitable part of the clothing business on Amazon, often ranging from 15% to 30%. To keep your seller account healthy, you must minimize these by providing accurate size charts and detailed product descriptions. Encourage customers to leave reviews, as social proof is vital for ranking. Once you have a winning product, use Amazon PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising to drive more traffic and increase sales velocity. As your brand grows, you can expand your product line with complementary styles, leveraging your established manufacturer relationship to maintain consistency across your collection.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical MOQ for Amazon private label clothing?

The typical MOQ for custom private label clothing from China is 100-300 pieces per color/style. Some manufacturers offer lower MOQs for in-stock styles that can be rebranded with your labels.

Do I need a trademark to sell private label clothing on Amazon?

While not strictly required to start, having a registered trademark allows you to enroll in Amazon Brand Registry, which provides access to A+ Content, Brand Stores, and better protection against counterfeiters.

How do I handle Amazon FBA packaging requirements?

Clothing must be placed in transparent polybags with a suffocation warning. Each bag must have a scannable FNSKU barcode. Your manufacturer in China can usually handle this for a small fee.

Should I use air or sea freight for Amazon FBA inventory?

Air freight is faster (5-10 days) but more expensive, suitable for testing new styles. Sea freight is much cheaper but takes 30-45 days, ideal for large restocks where margins are critical.

Can a manufacturer ship directly to Amazon FBA warehouses?

Yes, many Chinese manufacturers and freight forwarders offer DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) shipping directly to Amazon FBA centers, handling all customs clearance and local delivery.
