CANADA BUYER FAQ

Common sourcing questions

What is the minimum order?
Stock tests start from 2 pieces. Private-label and custom production typically starts at 100 pieces per color per style.
Can you prepare bilingual labels?
Yes, from buyer-approved artwork and wording. The Canadian buyer should verify current legal requirements and provide final label specifications.
How long does production take?
Sampling typically takes 7–12 business days and bulk production 20–35 business days after approval, before transit.
