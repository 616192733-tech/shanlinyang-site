Tracksuit Manufacturer in China: Wholesale Velour, Fleece & Knit Sets

PUBLISHED 2026-06-23 · SHANLINYANG APPAREL FACTORY TEAM · ~12 MIN READ

The tracksuit has undergone a massive transformation in recent years, evolving from gym-only wear to a staple of high-fashion and streetwear. Whether it's the 90s nostalgia of velour, the heavy-weight comfort of fleece, or the sophisticated look of fine-knit sets, tracksuits are a high-demand item for any modern apparel brand. If you are looking for a tracksuit manufacturer in China, you need a partner who can handle multiple fabric types and provide the high-quality finishes that today's consumers expect.

Shanlinyang Apparel, based in Xiamen, specializes in producing premium tracksuits and joggers for global markets. In this guide, we'll explore the different categories of tracksuits and the technical details you need to know when sourcing wholesale sets from China.

1. Choosing the Right Fabric for Your Tracksuit Line

The "feel" of a tracksuit is determined entirely by the fabric. As a leading tracksuit manufacturer, we offer several specialized materials:

Fleece (Cotton/Polyester Blend): The classic streetwear choice. We offer "brushed fleece" for a soft, fuzzy interior and "French Terry" for a cleaner, loop-back finish. Weights range from 280GSM to 450GSM (heavyweight).

Velour: For that iconic Y2K aesthetic. Our velour is plush, has a rich sheen, and is blended with Spandex for a comfortable stretch. It's perfect for boutique lounge collections.

Knit Fabrics: Fine-gauge knit tracksuits are a growing trend in luxury loungewear. They offer a more sophisticated, "put-together" look while remaining incredibly comfortable.

Tech-Fleece / Scuba: A modern, smooth, and structured fabric often used for high-performance or futuristic streetwear designs. It holds its shape perfectly and doesn't wrinkle easily.

2. Customization: Making Your Mark on Streetwear

In the crowded tracksuit market, branding is everything. A professional tracksuit manufacturer in China provides numerous ways to elevate your designs:

  • Embroidery: From flat embroidery to "3D puff" embroidery, this is the most durable and premium way to add your logo. It works exceptionally well on heavy fleece and velour.
  • Screen Printing and Puff Print: Ideal for bold, graphic designs on the back of hoodies or down the leg of joggers. Puff print adds a unique texture that is very popular in current streetwear trends.
  • Custom Hardware: We can provide custom-branded zipper pulls, aglets (the tips of drawstrings), and eyelets. Small details like these significantly increase the perceived value of the garment.
  • Woven Tapes and Piping: Adding branded tape down the sleeves or legs can give your tracksuits a classic "sport" or "retro" feel.

3. Sizing and Fit: From Slim to Oversized

The fit of a tracksuit is just as important as the fabric. As an experienced tracksuit manufacturer, we have master patterns for several distinct styles:

Oversized / Boxy: Currently the most popular fit in streetwear. It features dropped shoulders and a loose, relaxed silhouette.

Tapered / Slim-Fit: Often used for "tech" tracksuits or more traditional athletic wear. The joggers feature a narrow leg and a tight cuff at the ankle.

Custom Grading: We can grade your patterns for any market. We offer specialized sizing for the plus-size market, ensuring your tracksuits are comfortable and stylish up to 5XL.

4. Production and Quality Control

At Shanlinyang, we implement a rigorous QC process to ensure your bulk order is perfect:

  1. Fabric Inspection: We check for pilling resistance and colorfastness. You don't want your customers' hoodies to pill after the first wash.
  2. Symmetry Check: We ensure that logos are perfectly centered and that the legs and sleeves are of equal length.
  3. Stitching Durability: Tracksuits often experience stress at the pockets and crotch. We use reinforced stitching in these areas to prevent tearing.
  4. Weight Verification: We check the GSM of every fabric batch to ensure it matches the approved sample.

5. MOQs and Pricing for Wholesale Tracksuits

Finding a tracksuit manufacturer in China with flexible MOQs can be difficult. At Shanlinyang, we support boutique brands with MOQs starting at 100 sets per style.

Pricing: Wholesale prices typically range from $12 to $25 per set (FOB), depending on the fabric weight, complexity of the design, and total quantity.

Lead Times: Sampling takes 7-12 days. Bulk production takes 25-35 days. We offer fast air-freight options for urgent restocks and sea-freight for cost-effective volume orders.

FAQ: Sourcing Tracksuits from China

Can I mix and match sizes in my order?

Yes. Within our standard MOQ, you can typically select a range of sizes (e.g., S, M, L, XL) to meet your customers' needs.

What is the best fabric for a 'luxury' tracksuit?

We recommend a high-GSM cotton/polyester fleece or a fine-gauge knit blend. Heavyweight fabrics (380GSM+) always feel more 'luxury' to the consumer.

Do you offer eco-friendly tracksuit fabrics?

Yes. we can source organic cotton, recycled polyester fleece, and GRS-certified materials for sustainable collections.

How do I send my designs to you?

You can send us tech packs, sketches, or even photos of existing styles you like. Our pattern makers will work with you to create the perfect blueprint.

Can you help with custom labels and tags?

Absolutely. We can produce custom woven neck labels, care labels, and branded hang tags to ensure your brand identity is consistent throughout the product.
