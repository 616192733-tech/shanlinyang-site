Women's Dress Manufacturing at Shanlinyang

At Shanlinyang, we bring elegance and versatility to women's fashion through our specialized dress manufacturing services. Our production lines cover a wide spectrum of styles, including bodycon, wrap, maxi, evening, and sophisticated embroidered dresses. We work with a diverse range of premium materials such as lustrous satin, durable ponte knit, and eye-catching sequin fabrics. Our design team and skilled workers ensure that every dress reflects contemporary trends while maintaining superior craftsmanship. From casual day dresses to formal evening wear, we provide flexible customization options for labels of all sizes, ensuring your collection stands out in the competitive fashion market. Our attention to detail in stitching, fitting, and finishing makes us a preferred partner for global fashion brands.

Why Choose Our Women's Dress Collection?

  • Diverse fabric options: Ponte knit, Satin, Spandex blends, and high-quality sequins.
  • Flexible MOQs: 100pcs for custom designs, from 2pcs for stock items.
  • Comprehensive customization: Patterns, sizing, labeling, and specialized trims.
  • Reliable delivery: Samples in 7–12 days, bulk production in 20–35 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the MOQ for custom Women's Dresses?
Our custom MOQ is 100 pieces per color per style. For stock wholesale, you can order from just 2 pieces.
What fabrics are available for Women's Dresses?
We offer Ponte knit, Satin/polyester, Spandex blends, sequin fabric, and various lightweight summer materials.
How long does production take?
Sampling takes 7–12 business days. Bulk production is 20–35 business days after sample approval.
Can I add my own logo?
Yes. We offer screen printing, embroidery, woven labels, hangtags, and custom packaging.
Do you ship to the US and Europe?
Yes. We ship worldwide via DHL/FedEx/UPS air express and sea freight. DDP service available to US and EU.
