Bikini Manufacturer in China: Custom Swimwear Sets from 100 Pieces MOQ

The swimwear market is highly seasonal and trend-driven. To succeed, brands need a bikini manufacturer in China that can provide high-quality construction, sustainable fabric options, and low minimum order quantities.

Whether you're looking to launch a luxury beachwear label or a high-volume wholesale business, the quality of your swimwear is paramount. Bikinis are small garments, which means every stitch, seam, and hardware detail is under intense scrutiny. As an experienced manufacturer, we focus on the technical details that ensure a perfect fit and long-lasting durability.

1. Fabric Innovation: Sustainable and Performance Swimwear

In today's market, sustainability is no longer a niche—it's a requirement. As a leading bikini manufacturer in China, we have established relationships with the world's best fabric mills. We offer a wide range of recycled materials, including Repreve and Econyl. These fabrics are made from post-consumer waste like plastic bottles and fishing nets, yet they feel as soft and luxurious as traditional nylon.

Beyond sustainability, we focus on performance. Swimwear fabric must be resistant to chlorine, salt water, and UV rays. We use high-quality Italian and Chinese Lycra that maintains its shape and color even after a full summer of use. We also offer specialty fabrics like ribbed textures, velvets, and metallic finishes for brands looking to make a statement.

2. Masterful Fit and Construction

The biggest challenge in swimwear is the fit. A bikini must stay in place while swimming, sunbathing, and moving. We use advanced pattern-making software to ensure that our sizes are consistent and flattering. Our construction techniques include:

  • Seamless Edges: Zig-zag or cover-stitch finishes that lay flat against the skin without digging in.
  • Lining: We use high-quality linings that prevent the garment from becoming transparent when wet.
  • Cups and Padding: Options for removable pads or fixed molded cups to provide the desired shape and support.
  • Adjustability: Integrating high-quality sliders, hooks, and ties to allow for a custom fit.

3. Custom Printing and Embellishments

Bikinis are the perfect canvas for vibrant prints and unique designs. We offer several printing methods to bring your vision to life:

  • Digital Sublimation Printing: Ideal for complex, multi-colored patterns. It results in a soft hand-feel and will not crack or peel.
  • Screen Printing: Best for solid color logos or simple graphic designs.
  • Embellishments: We can add embroidery, sequins, beads, or custom metal hardware (tarnish-resistant) to elevate your designs.

4. Private Label Services for Swimwear Brands

Your brand story is told through your labels and packaging. As your private label bikini manufacturer in China, we provide:

  • Custom Woven Labels: High-definition labels that won't irritate the skin.
  • Heat-Transfer Tags: Clean, minimalist branding printed directly on the fabric.
  • Hygienic Liners: Branded liners that are essential for swimwear retail.
  • Branded Packaging: Reusable PVC bags or eco-friendly compostable bags that protect the product and enhance the brand image.

5. The Manufacturing Process and Low MOQ

Many swimwear brands struggle to find manufacturers who accept small orders. We have designed our production lines to be flexible. Our MOQ for custom bikinis starts at just 100 pieces per style. This can be split across sizes (e.g., S, M, L, XL), allowing you to offer a complete collection without over-ordering.

The Journey:

  1. Concept: You provide sketches or inspiration photos.
  2. Sampling: We create a prototype for fit and fabric approval.
  3. Production: Bulk manufacturing starts after your final sign-off.
  4. Quality Control: Each piece is inspected for seam strength, print accuracy, and measurement.
  5. Shipping: We handle logistics to the USA, Europe, and Australia via air or sea.

Frequently Asked Questions

Expert answers for brands sourcing swimwear from China.

What is the MOQ for custom bikini manufacturing?

Our MOQ for custom OEM bikini sets typically starts at 100 pieces per style/color. This can be split across multiple sizes to help brands offer a full range without excessive inventory.

Do you offer sustainable swimwear fabrics?

Yes, as a responsible bikini manufacturer in China, we source recycled fabrics like Repreve (made from plastic bottles) and Econyl (regenerated nylon from ocean waste).

Can you do custom digital printing on bikinis?

Absolutely. Digital printing is ideal for intricate patterns and vibrant colors. We also offer screen printing for simpler designs and foil printing for metallic effects.

How do you ensure swimwear is chlorine and salt-water resistant?

We source high-quality fabrics that are specifically treated for color fastness to chlorine and salt water. We also perform lab tests to ensure the elasticity doesn't degrade after repeated exposure.

Can I order bikini samples before bulk production?

Yes, we highly recommend ordering a 'proto sample' to check the fit and fabric. Once you are happy, we can produce a 'pre-production sample' for final approval before bulk.
