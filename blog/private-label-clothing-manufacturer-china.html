Private Label Clothing Manufacturer in China: The Complete 2025 Guide

PUBLISHED 2026-06-22 · SHANLINYANG APPAREL FACTORY TEAM · ~12 MIN READ

Launching a fashion brand in 2025 requires more than just good designs; it requires a strategic partnership with a reliable private label clothing manufacturer in China. With the global apparel market becoming increasingly competitive, the ability to produce high-quality, custom-branded garments at scale is the key to success for Shopify sellers, Amazon FBA brands, and boutique owners.

At Shanlinyang Apparel, we've helped hundreds of international buyers navigate the complexities of Chinese manufacturing. In this comprehensive guide, we'll break down everything you need to know about private labeling, from finding the right factory to understanding the true costs of production.

What is Private Label Clothing Manufacturing?

Private labeling is a business model where a manufacturer produces garments that are sold under a retailer's or brand's name. Unlike "off-the-shelf" wholesale, private label items feature your unique branding elements—labels, hangtags, packaging, and sometimes custom modifications to existing patterns.

In the context of private label clothing manufacturer China, there are two primary paths:

  • Full Custom (OEM): You provide the tech packs, fabric specifications, and patterns. The factory builds the garment from scratch.
  • Branded Styles (ODM): You choose from the factory's existing catalog of proven designs and add your logo, custom labels, and specific colors. This is the fastest way to launch a collection.

How to Find a Reliable Private Label Manufacturer in China

The search for the perfect partner often begins on platforms like Alibaba or Global Sources, but the real work happens during the vetting process. Here is how to filter the "middlemen" from the actual producers:

1. Verify Factory Status

Look for companies listed as "Verified Manufacturers." Ask for their business license and check their "Business Scope." A real factory will have "Manufacturing" or "Production" listed, not just "Trading."

2. Request Real-Time Proof

In 2025, any serious manufacturer should be willing to do a live video call via WeChat or WhatsApp to show you their production floor, sewing lines, and sample room. If they make excuses, they are likely a small trading company working out of an office.

3. Check Specializations

China is a vast country with regional hubs. For example, Xiamen (where Shanlinyang is based) is a world-renowned hub for high-end knitwear, activewear, and yoga sets. If you want yoga leggings, don't look for a factory that primarily makes denim jackets.

Understanding MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity) in 2025

One of the biggest hurdles for new brands is the MOQ. In the past, Chinese factories demanded 1,000+ pieces per style. However, the rise of "Small Batch" manufacturing has changed the landscape.

The Costs: Beyond the Unit Price

When you get a quote from a private label clothing manufacturer in China, it's usually an FOB (Free On Board) or EXW (Ex Works) price. To calculate your true Landed Cost, you must account for:

  1. Sample Fees: Typically 2–3x the bulk unit price. This covers pattern making and small-scale fabric sourcing. (At Shanlinyang, we often refund this on bulk orders).
  2. Branding Accessories: Custom woven labels, heat-transfer logos, and printed polybags.
  3. Shipping: Air express (DHL/FedEx) is fast but expensive; sea freight is economical for large volumes but takes 30–40 days.
  4. Import Duties: Check your local HS codes for apparel to estimate taxes.

The Step-by-Step Production Process

Success lies in the details. Here is how a professional order flows through our factory:

  • Step 1: Consultation & Quoting: Send your designs or choose from our catalog. We provide a preliminary price.
  • Step 2: Sampling: We create a physical prototype. You check the fit, fabric feel, and sewing quality.
  • Step 3: Bulk Deposit: Once the sample is approved, you pay the 30% deposit.
  • Step 4: Sourcing & Cutting: We source the bulk fabric and begin the precision cutting process.
  • Step 5: Sewing & Branding: Your garments are assembled and your private labels are attached.
  • Step 6: QC & Packing: Every piece undergoes a 100% inspection for loose threads, stains, or size discrepancies.
  • Step 7: Balance & Shipping: You receive photos/videos of the finished goods, pay the balance, and we ship.

FAQ: Private Labeling in China

What is the difference between Private Label and White Label?

White label refers to generic products sold to multiple retailers. Private label implies more customization and exclusivity to your brand, even if the base design is from the factory.

Do I need a tech pack to start?

A tech pack is ideal, but if you have a physical sample or even high-resolution photos with a detailed size chart, our pattern makers can develop the style for you.

How can I protect my designs?

We sign Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) with our clients to ensure your unique designs and patterns are not sold to other buyers.

Can you handle dropshipping?

While we are a bulk manufacturer, we can provide "blind shipping" for large-scale influencers or assist in logistics to your 3PL warehouse in the US or Europe.

What are the most popular private label items right now?

Currently, two-piece lounge sets, seamless yoga wear, and high-quality basics (heavyweight tees) are the top performers for our clients.
