Activewear & Yoga Wear Manufacturing at Shanlinyang

Shanlinyang Apparel specializes in high-performance activewear and yoga wear for global brands. Our collections feature squat-proof leggings, sports bras, crop tops, and coordinated yoga sets crafted from premium 4-way stretch fabrics including Nylon/Spandex and Polyester/Spandex blends. With a custom MOQ of just 100 pieces per color per style, we're the ideal manufacturing partner for DTC brands, Shopify sellers, and boutique activewear labels looking to launch or scale their collections.

Why Source Activewear from Shanlinyang?

  • Squat-proof opacity testing on all leggings
  • 4-way stretch with 80%+ recovery rate
  • Moisture-wicking and quick-dry finishes available
  • Custom MOQ from 100pcs — ideal for new brand launches
  • Seamless and cut-and-sew options

Activewear FAQ

What is the MOQ for custom activewear?
100 pieces per color per style for custom orders. Stock wholesale starts from 2 pieces.
What fabrics do you use for yoga wear?
We use Nylon/Spandex (80/20), Polyester/Spandex, and recycled fabric options. All activewear undergoes squat-proof opacity testing.
Can I get seamless activewear?
Yes, we offer both seamless knit and cut-and-sew activewear options.
How long does activewear production take?
Sampling: 7–12 business days. Bulk: 20–35 business days after sample approval.
Do you ship activewear to the USA?
Yes, we ship worldwide via DHL/FedEx/UPS. DDP service available to the US and EU.
