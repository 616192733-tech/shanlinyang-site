WHAT TO ATTACH

Files and details that speed up quoting

Best attachments

  • Tech pack or measurement chart
  • Front/back product photos
  • Fabric composition or hand-feel reference
  • Logo vector file for print or embroidery
  • Packaging or label references

If you do not have a tech pack

  • Send 2–5 reference photos
  • Tell us what you want to change
  • Share target price range if available
  • Choose a target market: US, Canada, Europe or other
  • Confirm whether you need standard or plus-size grading
