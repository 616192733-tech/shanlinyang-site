Why yoga wear needs a focused supplier

Yoga clothing is not just “stretch fabric plus a logo.”

The best-selling yoga products must feel soft, stay opaque, recover after stretching, hold shape at the waist, and keep logos intact after repeated washing. A practical factory route reduces fit complaints, returns and launch delays.

FAQ

Questions buyers ask before sourcing yoga clothing

Can Shanlinyang make private-label yoga clothing from photos?

Yes. Reference photos, product links, sketches or samples are enough to begin a feasibility review. A tech pack helps when exact measurements, construction and grading are already decided.

What is the MOQ for private-label yoga wear?

Custom yoga wear usually starts from about 100 pieces per color per style, depending on fabric, logo method, trims and packaging. Stock-based testing may support smaller quantities.

Can I use my own logo and packaging?

Yes. Logo methods can include heat transfer, silicone print, reflective print or embroidery where suitable. Shanlinyang can also support woven labels, care labels, hangtags and branded poly bags.

Can you prepare yoga wear orders for US/EU delivery?

Yes. Delivery planning can include DDP options, Amazon FBA preparation, barcode labels, carton marks, size/color separation and 3PL warehouse delivery requirements.
