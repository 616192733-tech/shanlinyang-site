Frequently Asked Questions

What fabrics are best for Valentine's Day collections?

Silk, lace, satin, and soft mesh are ideal for creating romantic and feminine styles.

When should I launch my Valentine's Day line?

Aim to launch by mid-January to capture early shoppers looking for the perfect date-night outfit.

Do manufacturers provide heart-shaped embroidery?

Yes, many factories in China specialize in detailed embroidery and applique for themed collections.

What is the best shipping method for small Valentine's drops?

Express air shipping (DHL/FedEx) is recommended for small, time-sensitive collections.

Can I get samples before committing to a full Valentine's order?

Absolutely, reputable manufacturers always offer sampling to ensure style and fit accuracy.
