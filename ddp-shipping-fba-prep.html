BUYER QUESTIONS

DDP and FBA answers

Can you ship directly to Amazon?
Yes, if the order has the correct barcode labels, carton marks and warehouse instructions before packing. Amazon requirements should be shared early to avoid relabeling delays.
Can you ship to my 3PL warehouse instead of Amazon?
Yes. Many brands first ship to a 3PL warehouse for inspection, relabeling, bundling or multi-channel fulfillment.
Can you quote landed cost?
For many routes, Shanlinyang can help estimate DDP landed cost. Final pricing depends on order volume, carton dimensions, destination, duties and route availability.
What should I confirm before bulk packing?
Confirm size ratio, barcode files, bag warnings, carton label format, maximum carton weight, SKU separation, destination details and delivery deadline.
