SERVICE — OEM · ODM · PRIVATE LABEL

Custom Clothing Manufacturer for Women's Fashion Brands

We turn tech packs, sketches and reference photos into branded, retail-ready garments — from 100 pieces per color per style. This page explains exactly how our custom program works, what it costs to start, and what you need to send us.

PROGRAM 1

Private label on stock styles

The fastest route: pick any style from our catalog, we apply your logo, woven labels, hangtags and packaging. MOQ 100pcs per color per style; no development fee.

PROGRAM 2

OEM from your tech pack

You own the design; we manufacture it exactly to spec. Send measurements, fabric requirements and artwork — we quote within 24h and sample in 7–12 days.

PROGRAM 3

ODM from a sketch or photo

No tech pack? Our pattern room develops the design from a sketch or reference photo, including fabric sourcing and fit grading for your market.

CUSTOM PROGRAM FAQ

Questions brands ask before their first run

What does 100pcs per color per style mean in practice?
If you order one style in two colors, the minimum is 200 pieces total (100 of each color). Sizes within each color can be split across a size curve, e.g. S/M/L/XL or XL–5XL.
How much does a custom sample cost?
Sample fees depend on fabric and complexity, typically USD 30–80 per piece, and are refunded against your bulk order. Sampling takes 7–12 days plus express shipping.
Do you sign NDAs and protect our designs?
Yes. We sign NDAs on request and never resell customer-exclusive designs, prints or branded trims to other buyers.
What payment terms do you accept?
Standard terms are 30% deposit and 70% balance before shipment, via T/T bank transfer, Alibaba Trade Assurance, PayPal or Wise for smaller orders.
Can you handle US and EU compliance labeling?
Yes — fiber content, country of origin, care symbols and CA Prop 65 / EU textile regulation labeling can all be produced to your market's requirements.
