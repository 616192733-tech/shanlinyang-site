Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start preparing for Black Friday inventory?

Ideally, you should start planning at least 4-6 months in advance to account for production and shipping.

What is the typical lead time for Chinese clothing manufacturers during peak season?

Lead times can increase by 2-4 weeks during peak season, so expect 6-10 weeks for production.

How can I avoid stockouts during BFCM?

Implement a buffer stock strategy and work closely with your manufacturer on real-time inventory tracking.

What fabrics are best for Black Friday winter collections?

Heavyweight cotton, wool blends, and velvet are popular choices for winter festive wear.

Should I use air freight or sea freight for last-minute inventory?

Air freight is faster but more expensive; use it only for high-demand items that need immediate replenishment.
