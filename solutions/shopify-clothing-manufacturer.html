A practical launch path

  1. Pick hero styles

    Choose products from activewear, sets, dresses, plus size or swimwear collections.

  2. Approve samples

    Review fit, fabric handfeel, logo position and packing requirements before bulk.

  3. Produce the first drop

    Use low-MOQ custom production or stock tests depending on your launch risk.

  4. Restock winners

    Repeat approved styles faster with size/color data from your Shopify orders.
