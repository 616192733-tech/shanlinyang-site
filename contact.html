What to include

Four details that make a factory quote much more accurate

1. Style reference

Send a photo, sketch, product link, or tech pack. Even a rough idea helps us understand pattern complexity and sewing details.

2. Fabric direction

Tell us whether you need stretch, compression, lining, recycled fabric, soft handfeel, or a fabric sample to match.

3. Quantity & sizes

Share estimated pieces per style, color, and size range. This affects MOQ, cutting efficiency, and unit price.

4. Destination & deadline

North America, Europe, and Australia each have different logistics planning. A target launch date helps us suggest a realistic route.

Quote questions

Common questions before contacting us

Can you help if I only have reference photos?

Yes. Reference photos are enough for an early discussion. For production-ready pricing, we may ask for measurements, fabric direction, sample requirements, or a tech pack.

Do you support small brands?

Yes. We work with online brands, boutique buyers, and startup labels. The right route depends on whether you need custom OEM/ODM development or lower-risk wholesale testing.

Can you ship to the United States or Europe?

Yes. Many enquiries are for North America and Europe. Please include destination country and any marketplace or warehouse requirement so we can plan packing and logistics early.

What happens after I submit the form?

We review your style, quantity, and deadline, then reply with practical next steps: sample route, MOQ, fabric questions, estimated feasibility, or documents needed for accurate pricing.
