Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most popular colors for NYE fashion?

Classic gold, silver, emerald green, and deep black remain the top choices for New Year's Eve.

How difficult is it to manufacture sequined garments?

Sequined items require specialized machinery and skilled labor, so choose a factory with experience in evening wear.

When is the best time to start NYE production?

Plan to finish production by mid-October to allow for shipping and holiday marketing.

Are MOQs higher for sequined fabrics?

Sometimes, as specialized fabrics may have their own minimums from the textile mills.

Can I do a mixed collection of party wear?

Yes, most manufacturers allow you to mix styles (e.g., dresses, tops, skirts) within your total order.
