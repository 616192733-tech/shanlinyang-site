Women's Loungewear Manufacturer in China: Soft Sets, Robes & Cozy Basics

PUBLISHED 2026-06-30 · SHANLINYANG APPAREL FACTORY TEAM · ~12 MIN READ

The global shift toward home-based work and a renewed focus on self-care has transformed the loungewear industry. No longer confined to the bedroom, modern loungewear blends style, comfort, and versatility, creating a massive opportunity for fashion brands. Finding a reliable women's loungewear manufacturer in China is the first step toward launching a successful collection that resonates with today's comfort-seeking consumers.

In this guide, we’ll explore the essential aspects of sourcing loungewear, from fabric selection and manufacturing techniques to navigating MOQs and private label customization. At Shanlinyang Apparel, we’ve spent years perfecting the art of cozy apparel, helping brands worldwide deliver the softness their customers crave.

1. Why Source Loungewear from China?

China remains the premier destination for loungewear manufacturing for several reasons. First and foremost is the unmatched access to specialized fabrics. The provinces of Guangdong, Zhejiang, and Fujian are home to some of the world's most advanced textile mills, specializing in ultra-soft blends like modal, bamboo, and premium organic cotton.

Furthermore, Chinese manufacturers have integrated sophisticated supply chains that allow for rapid prototyping and efficient production. Whether you are looking for intricate lace-trimmed robes or minimalist rib-knit sets, the expertise in handling delicate fabrics is unparalleled. The combination of technological advancement and competitive pricing makes China the logical choice for both startups and established fashion houses.

2. Popular Loungewear Fabrics: From Modal to French Terry

The success of a loungewear brand depends almost entirely on the "hand-feel" of the products. Choosing the right fabric is critical. Here are the top contenders in the loungewear market:

Modal and Micromodal

Known for its incredible softness and silky drape, modal is a type of rayon made from beech tree cellulose. It is highly breathable, resistant to shrinkage, and holds color beautifully. It’s perfect for nightgowns and lightweight pajama sets.

Bamboo Viscose

Bamboo is highly favored for its sustainable profile and moisture-wicking properties. It feels cool to the touch and is naturally hypoallergenic, making it ideal for consumers with sensitive skin.

French Terry and Fleece

For cozy hoodies, joggers, and robes, French terry and brushed fleece offer the warmth and weight needed for cooler seasons. High-quality manufacturers ensure that the "loop" side of the terry is smooth and the "brushed" side of the fleece remains soft after multiple washes.

Rib-Knit Fabrics

Ribbed textures add a premium, sophisticated look to simple loungewear silhouettes. They provide excellent stretch and recovery, ensuring that leggings and tops maintain their shape throughout the day.

3. Our Loungewear Manufacturing Capabilities

At Shanlinyang Apparel, we offer a comprehensive range of loungewear styles. Our factory is equipped with specialized machinery to handle soft, stretchy fabrics without causing puckering or uneven seams.

Matching Loungewear Sets

Two-piece sets consisting of wide-leg pants and oversized tops or camisoles and shorts are our most popular items. We ensure perfect color matching across different fabric weights and textures.

Robes and Wraps

From lightweight satin robes to heavy plush hotel-style wraps, we can manufacture a variety of silhouettes with custom belt loops, pockets, and decorative trims.

Cozy Basics and Sleepwear

We produce high-quality basics including tanks, tees, and leggings designed specifically for relaxation. Our sleepwear lines include button-down pajama sets and slip dresses with adjustable straps.

4. Customization and Private Labeling for Lounge Brands

Standing out in the loungewear market requires a unique brand identity. We provide extensive customization options to help you create a cohesive and recognizable collection.

Custom Branding and Labels

We can produce custom woven labels, printed neck labels (for tagless comfort), and high-quality hangtags. Branded drawstrings and hardware can also be sourced to match your aesthetic.

Custom Dyeing (Pantone Matching)

If you have a specific color palette in mind, we can custom-dye fabrics to match your Pantone codes. This ensures your brand has a unique look that can't be found in off-the-shelf stock fabrics.

Embroidery and Screen Printing

Add logos or slogans to your loungewear using various techniques. For loungewear, we often recommend soft-hand screen printing or delicate tonal embroidery to maintain the garment's comfort.

5. Navigating MOQ, Pricing, and Lead Times

Understanding the business side of manufacturing is essential for planning your launch. We aim to be a flexible partner for brands at all stages.

Minimum Order Quantities (MOQ)

For custom OEM designs, our MOQ typically starts at 100 pieces per color per style. For brands just starting out, we also offer a selection of high-quality "blank" loungewear that can be ordered in smaller quantities and customized with your logo.

Pricing Factors

The unit price is determined by the fabric choice, design complexity, and total quantity. Generally, premium fabrics like bamboo or modal will have a higher price point than standard cotton-poly blends. We provide transparent, itemized quotes so you know exactly where your budget is going.

Production Timeline

A typical production cycle includes 7-12 days for sampling and 25-35 days for bulk production. We recommend allowing 4-6 months from initial design to market launch to ensure every detail is perfect.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the minimum order for custom loungewear?
For custom OEM loungewear, our MOQ usually starts at 100 pieces per color per style. For in-stock items, we support smaller wholesale orders.

Q2: Can you help with fabric sourcing for specific textures?
Yes, we have a vast fabric library and can source specific weights and textures, including eco-friendly options like bamboo and recycled fibers.

Q3: Do you provide samples before bulk production?
Absolutely. We create a "Pre-Production Sample" (PPS) for your approval to ensure the fit, fabric, and color meet your specifications before we start bulk sewing.

Q4: Can you manufacture plus-size loungewear?
Yes, we specialize in inclusive sizing from XS up to 5XL. We understand that comfort is for every body type.

Q5: How do you handle quality control for soft fabrics?
We use specialized needles and tension settings for soft knits. Every garment undergoes a 100% inspection for seam strength, measurements, and fabric defects.
