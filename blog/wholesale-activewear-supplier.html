Wholesale Activewear Supplier in China: Stock & Custom Options for Retailers

The global activewear market is booming, and for retailers, finding the right wholesale activewear supplier in China is the key to unlocking high margins and customer loyalty. Whether you are looking to stock a boutique with the latest trends or launch a full-scale fitness brand, having a reliable supply chain is non-negotiable.

Shanlinyang Apparel serves as both a high-end manufacturer and a flexible wholesale partner. We offer a dual approach: a curated selection of ready-to-ship "stock" styles for immediate needs, and full "custom" OEM services for brands that want to innovate. In this guide, we’ll help you understand how to maximize your partnership with a Chinese activewear supplier.

1. The Best of Both Worlds: Stock vs. Custom Production

As a retailer, your needs change depending on the season and your growth stage.

  • Stock Wholesale: This is ideal for quickly filling gaps in your inventory. We maintain a rotating collection of high-demand leggings, sports bras, and matching sets. These are available with low MOQs and fast shipping, perfect for testing new colors or styles without a large upfront investment.
  • Custom OEM: For brands looking to create a unique identity, custom production allows you to specify every detail—from fabric composition to pocket placement and branding. This requires longer lead times but results in a product that is exclusively yours.
Most of our successful clients use a hybrid approach, carrying core stock items while developing unique "hero" products through our custom services.

2. Quality Assurance: Technical Specs for High-End Fitness Wear

When sourcing wholesale activewear in China, quality is what separates the winners from the losers. Your customers expect "squat-proof" leggings, non-see-through fabrics, and seams that don't chafe. We use premium nylon/spandex blends that offer 4-way stretch and excellent recovery.

Our manufacturing process includes high-density flat-lock stitching, which provides both strength and a smooth finish against the skin. We also conduct rigorous "stretch tests" and "wash tests" on all our wholesale styles to ensure they perform under pressure. When you buy from Shanlinyang, you are buying factory-direct quality that rivals premium retail brands.

3. White Labeling: Turning Wholesale into Your Brand

You don't always need to design a product from scratch to have a "branded" collection. Our white label service allows you to take our proven wholesale designs and add your own branding. This includes:

  • Heat-Transfer Logos: Clean, durable, and professional-looking.
  • Custom Hangtags: Personalized with your brand's story and pricing.
  • Woven Neck Labels: Replacing generic labels with your brand's identity.
  • Custom Packaging: Branded frosted zip-lock bags for a premium unboxing experience.
This is the fastest and most cost-effective way to launch a branded activewear line with minimal risk.

4. Navigating MOQs and Pricing for Better Margins

The biggest advantage of working with a wholesale activewear supplier China is the pricing. By cutting out the middleman and buying direct from the factory source, you can significantly increase your profit margins. Our wholesale pricing is tiered, meaning the more you buy, the lower the price per unit.

We keep our MOQs flexible. For stock items, you can often mix and match sizes and colors to meet a small minimum. This agility is crucial for small-to-medium retailers who need to stay "lean" with their inventory. For custom orders, we work with you to find a production volume that makes financial sense for your business.

5. Logistics: From Xiamen to Your Warehouse

Getting your wholesale order delivered shouldn't be a headache. We provide comprehensive shipping solutions, whether you need a single box sent via DHL or a full container via sea freight. We handle the export documentation and can provide DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) quotes so you know exactly what your landed cost will be.

We also provide high-quality marketing materials for many of our wholesale items. This includes professional studio photography and lifestyle shots, allowing you to get your products listed on your website as soon as you place your order. Our goal is to make the entire process—from sourcing to sales—as seamless as possible.

Conclusion

Finding a reliable wholesale activewear supplier in China is a strategic move that can transform your retail business. With a focus on quality, flexibility, and branding, Shanlinyang Apparel is dedicated to helping you scale. Explore our wholesale options and start building your fitness empire today.
