FAQ

Private-label activewear questions

Can I start activewear production without a tech pack?

Yes. You can start with reference photos, product links, sketches, or samples. A tech pack improves precision, but early feasibility review can begin with clear references and quantity goals.

What MOQ should I expect?

Custom activewear production often starts from about 100 pieces per color per style, depending on fabric, color, logo method, trims, and packaging. Stock-based testing can start lower.

Can you help with private-label logos and packaging?

Yes. Shanlinyang can support heat-transfer logos, embroidery, woven labels, printed care labels, hangtags, branded poly bags, barcode labels, and carton marks.

Do you support US and European delivery?

Yes. Delivery planning can include air, sea, DDP options, Amazon FBA preparation, barcode labels, carton marks, and 3PL warehouse delivery preparation.
