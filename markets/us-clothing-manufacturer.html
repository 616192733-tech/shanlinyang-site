TWO WAYS TO START

Test first, then scale

Stock-style test orders

Start from 2 pieces on available styles. Mix products to assess fabric, fit and customer response before committing to custom production.

Private label and custom production

Add your logo, labels, hangtags and packaging to an existing style, or manufacture from your tech pack, sketch, photo or reference sample. Custom MOQ typically starts at 100 pieces per color per style.

US-MARKET REQUIREMENTS

Specifications buyers can approve before bulk

US size grading

Use a supplied brand chart or review a proposed XS–XXL chart. Plus-size programs can use dedicated XL–5XL blocks rather than simply enlarging straight sizes.

Brand-ready packaging

Options include woven labels, care labels, hangtags, poly bags, barcode labels and carton marks. Requirements are confirmed before production.

Documented QC

Fabric, inline production and final inspection checkpoints can be documented with photos or video. Activewear programs can include opacity and stretch checks.

DELIVERY

Shipping options for US buyers

Choose express, air freight or sea freight based on launch date, volume and budget. DDP delivery may be available for suitable orders, while Amazon and third-party fulfillment shipments can be prepared to the buyer's labeling and carton requirements.

Final shipping terms, duties, transit estimates and destination requirements are confirmed in the quotation. Buyers remain responsible for verifying product-specific US labeling and compliance obligations.

PRODUCTION PROCESS

From reference to US delivery

  1. Send requirements

    Share a style number, tech pack or reference image, plus quantity, sizes, destination and launch date.

  2. Approve quote and sample

    Review unit price, MOQ, lead time and branding. Approve a physical sample before bulk cutting.

  3. Produce and inspect

    Bulk production typically takes 20–35 business days after approval, with agreed QC checkpoints.

  4. Pack and ship

    Approve packing marks and shipping method, then receive documents and tracking or freight details.

US BUYER FAQ

Common sourcing questions

What is the MOQ for US clothing brands?
Stock styles can be tested from 2 pieces. Private-label and custom production typically starts at 100 pieces per color per style.
Can you manufacture to US sizing?
Yes. We can work from your approved size chart or prepare a proposed chart for sample approval before bulk production.
Can you prepare orders for Amazon FBA?
Available services include barcode labels, individual packaging and carton marks. Send the current fulfillment requirements with your inquiry so they can be quoted and confirmed.
How quickly can I receive a quote?
Complete inquiries are normally answered within one business day. Include quantity, destination, target sizes, branding and reference files for a more accurate first quote.

Plan your US-market production run

Send your target quantity, destination, size range and a product reference. We will reply with MOQ, pricing, sampling and lead-time details.
