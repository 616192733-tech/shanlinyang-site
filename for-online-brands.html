WHO WE SERVE — FOR SHOPIFY & AMAZON BRANDS

Private label manufacturing for Shopify and Amazon clothing brands

You handle the storefront and the marketing; we handle everything between your tech pack and your warehouse — branded, barcoded and inspection-passed.

E-commerce-ready out of the carton

Every unit packed in a poly bag with your branding, FNSKU/UPC barcode labels applied for FBA, carton labels to Amazon spec, and size-sticker placement that survives prep inspection. WFS and Shopify 3PL prep also supported.

Private label from 100 pieces

Your logo (print or embroidery), woven neck labels, custom hangtags and packaging from 100 pcs per color per style — small enough to validate a niche, branded enough to build review equity that resellers of generic stock can't touch.

Photo QC before anything ships

Final AQL inspection with a photographed report sent for your approval before balance payment — measurements, stitching close-ups, label placement, packaging. Third-party inspection (SGS or your agent) welcome.

Listing-ready product data

We supply fabric composition, care instructions, measurement charts per size and product weights — everything your listing, size chart widget and customs declaration need, in one spec sheet.

FAQ — FOR SHOPIFY & AMAZON BRANDS

FAQ

Can you ship directly to Amazon FBA warehouses?
Yes — DDP door-to-door to US/EU FBA warehouses with Amazon-compliant carton labels and prep. You never touch the inventory.
Do you protect my designs from being resold?
Yes. We sign NDAs on request and customer-exclusive designs, prints and trims are never offered to other buyers.
What if my first design doesn't fit right?
Sampling includes a revision round; bulk doesn't start until you approve the fit on an actual garment.
