Plus Size Clothing Manufacturing (XL–5XL)

Shanlinyang Apparel offers dedicated plus-size manufacturing with genuine curve-graded patterns from XL to 5XL — not simply scaled-up straight sizes. Our plus-size range includes activewear, two-piece sets, dresses, tracksuits, and swimwear, all designed with real curve blocks that ensure proper fit and comfort for plus-size customers.

Why Our Plus Size Manufacturing Stands Out

  • Genuine curve-graded blocks from XL to 5XL
  • Dedicated plus-size patterns — not scaled-up straight sizes
  • Full range: activewear, sets, dresses, swimwear, tracksuits
  • 4-way stretch fabrics for comfort and recovery
  • Custom MOQ from 100pcs per color per style

Plus Size FAQ

What plus sizes do you manufacture?
We manufacture from XL to 5XL using dedicated curve-graded patterns.
Are your plus size patterns graded or scaled up?
Our plus-size patterns are genuinely curve-graded on real plus-size blocks, not scaled-up from straight sizes.
What is the MOQ for custom plus size clothing?
100 pieces per color per style. Stock plus-size styles from 2 pieces.
What fabrics work best for plus size activewear?
We recommend 4-way stretch Nylon/Spandex or Polyester/Spandex for maximum comfort and recovery.
Can I order mixed sizes in one batch?
Yes, you can specify a size ratio within your order (e.g., 20% XL, 30% 2XL, etc.).
