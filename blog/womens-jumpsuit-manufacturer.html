Women's Jumpsuit Manufacturer in China: Wholesale Rompers & One-Piece Styles

PUBLISHED 2026-06-30 · SHANLINYANG APPAREL FACTORY TEAM · ~14 MIN READ

Jumpsuits and rompers have evolved from trend-based "statement pieces" into essential wardrobe staples. Their "all-in-one" convenience, combined with their ability to be dressed up or down, makes them a high-demand item for boutiques and online fashion brands. However, from a manufacturing standpoint, jumpsuits are one of the most complex garments to produce, requiring precise pattern-making to ensure a comfortable fit across varying torso lengths.

If you are looking for a women's jumpsuit manufacturer in China, you need a partner who understands the technical nuances of one-piece construction. At Shanlinyang Apparel, we specialize in high-quality jumpsuit and romper production, providing fashion brands with the expertise needed to launch successful collections.

1. The Technical Challenges of Jumpsuit Manufacturing

Unlike separate tops and bottoms, a jumpsuit must accommodate the entire vertical length of the body. This makes "fit" the most critical factor. A jumpsuit that is too short in the torso will be uncomfortable, while one that is too long will look baggy and ill-fitting. A professional manufacturer must have expert pattern makers who understand "rise" and "torso" measurements for different global markets.

In addition to fit, the placement of zippers, buttons, and elastic waistbands is crucial for both aesthetics and functionality. We utilize advanced 3D fitting software and extensive physical sampling to ensure our jumpsuits provide a flattering silhouette while remaining practical for the wearer. Sourcing from an experienced China manufacturer ensures you get the benefit of specialized machinery and skilled labor required for these complex garments.

2. Jumpsuit and Romper Styles We Specialize In

Our factory handles a wide variety of one-piece silhouettes, from structured professional wear to relaxed holiday rompers.

Casual Rompers and Playsuits

Ideal for summer collections, these short one-pieces often feature elastic waists, tie-shoulders, and breathable fabrics like linen or cotton gauze. We focus on ensuring the leg openings are comfortable and the finish is clean.

Structured Utility Jumpsuits

Also known as boiler suits, these pieces feature a more "workwear" inspired look with collars, button-down fronts, and multiple pockets. These require heavier fabrics like denim or canvas and precise top-stitching.

Elegant Evening Jumpsuits

Designed for special occasions, these sets often use wide-leg trousers and intricate top designs (like halter necks or one-shoulder styles). We utilize premium fabrics like crepe, satin, and velvet, often adding internal lining for a luxury finish.

Active and Yoga Jumpsuits

Often called "unitards" or "onesies," these are made from high-stretch performance fabrics. They require specialized sewing techniques like flatlock stitching to ensure the seams don't chafe during movement.

3. Fabric Selection for One-Piece Garments

The fabric choice determines how the jumpsuit drapes and how comfortable it feels throughout the day. Here are the most popular options we source for our clients:

Viscose and Rayon Blends

These fabrics offer a beautiful drape and a soft feel, making them perfect for wide-leg jumpsuits that need to move with the wearer. They take prints exceptionally well, allowing for vibrant floral or geometric designs.

Linen and Cotton Blends

For a structured yet breathable look, linen-cotton blends are the gold standard. They provide a sophisticated "natural" look that is highly popular in boutique fashion.

Polyester Crepe and Scuba

These synthetic fabrics are durable, wrinkle-resistant, and offer a bit of "hold," which is excellent for evening wear or professional jumpsuits that need to maintain a sharp silhouette.

Sustainable Options

We also offer eco-friendly fabrics including Tencel (Lyocell) and recycled polyester, helping your brand meet the growing demand for sustainable fashion.

4. Customization and Private Labeling Services

Building a brand requires more than just a great design; it requires consistent branding across your entire product line.

Hardware Customization

Jumpsuits often rely on functional hardware like zippers and buttons. We can source custom-branded metal buttons, unique zip-pulls, and high-quality buckles to give your garments a signature look.

Custom Waistbands and Belts

Whether you want a built-in drawstring, an elasticated back, or a separate matching fabric belt, we can customize the waist detail to perfectly complement your design.

Labeling and Tags

We offer full private label services, including woven main labels, care labels, and custom hangtags. For jumpsuits, we recommend placing care labels in discreet locations that don't interfere with the garment's fit.

5. MOQ, Pricing, and Lead Times for Jumpsuits

Manufacturing jumpsuits requires a significant amount of fabric and labor per unit, which influences the pricing and logistics.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Our MOQ for custom OEM jumpsuits is typically 100 pieces per color per style. This is a very competitive minimum for such a complex garment, allowing brands to launch multiple styles within a single season. For wholesale stock items, the minimum is as low as 2 pieces.

Pricing Factors

Because jumpsuits use almost twice as much fabric as a standard top, the unit price is higher. FOB prices typically range from US$8 to US$18 depending on the fabric and design complexity. We provide detailed quotes to help you manage your margins effectively.

Production Timeline

Due to the complexity of the pattern-making and sewing process, jumpsuits have a slightly longer lead time. Sampling takes 7-12 days, and bulk production typically takes 30-40 days. We recommend planning your collection at least 5 months in advance of your launch date.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the MOQ for custom jumpsuits?
Our standard MOQ for custom OEM jumpsuits is 100 pieces per color per style. For in-stock wholesale items, the minimum is 2 pieces.

Q2: Can you manufacture jumpsuits from my tech pack?
Yes, we are a full-service OEM factory. We can work directly from your tech packs or recreate a style from a reference sample.

Q3: How do you ensure the fit of a one-piece garment?
We use professional grade pattern-making software and conduct multiple fit tests during the sampling phase to ensure the torso and rise are perfectly proportioned.

Q4: Do you offer different trouser lengths?
Yes, we can manufacture jumpsuits in petite, regular, or tall lengths based on your target market's requirements.

Q5: Can you source specific hardware like custom buckles?
Absolutely. We have a wide network of trim and hardware suppliers and can source or manufacture custom components for your brand.
