WHO WE SERVE FOR FITNESS & ATHLEISURE LABELS

The activewear factory for gyms, coaches and athleisure startups

Sheer leggings kill fitness brands faster than any ad account ban. We build activewear lines on measured opacity, tested stretch recovery and branding that survives the wash cycle.

Squat-proof is a measurement, not a slogan

Bottoms run 22060 gsm spandex blends with opacity checked at QC; every pre-production sample ships with a stretch-and-opacity report so you approve numbers, not promises.

Capsule collections that launch a brand

The proven structure: one hero set (bra + legging or crop + flare), one cover layer, two colorways 3 styles × 100 pcs each is a complete brand launch, not a sample drawer. We help plan the size curve from your audience data.

Branding that survives the gym

Silicone heat transfers on waistbands (50+ wash tested), flat embroidery on fleece layers, reflective prints for running lines all included in custom pricing from 100 pcs.

Gyms & coaching merch programs

For gyms and online coaches selling merch to members: we hold your approved styles on file so restocks are a one-line WhatsApp message, with mixed-size repeat runs from 100 pcs.

FAQ FOR FITNESS & ATHLEISURE LABELS

FAQ

Which fabrics do you recommend for leggings?
Nylon-spandex (76/24) for premium buttery lines; poly-spandex (82/18) where custom prints matter. Both available from the 100pc custom MOQ.
Can you do all-over custom prints?
Yes sublimation all-over prints and burnout florals are produced in-house from 10000 pcs depending on the base fabric.
How fast can a 3-style capsule launch?
Typical timeline: quotes week 1, samples weeks 2, revision week 4, bulk weeks 5, air delivery week 10.
