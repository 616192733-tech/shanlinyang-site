Women's Tank Top Manufacturer in China: Custom Basics, Racerbacks & Longline Styles

PUBLISHED 2026-06-30 · SHANLINYANG APPAREL FACTORY TEAM · ~11 MIN READ

Tank tops are the ultimate versatile garment. From base layers and gym essentials to stylish summer staples and professional camisoles, the humble tank top is a high-volume category for any fashion brand. However, achieving the perfect fit and choosing the right fabric is what separates a best-seller from a product that sits on the shelf. If you are looking for a women's tank top manufacturer in China, you need a partner who can balance high-volume efficiency with meticulous attention to detail.

At Shanlinyang Apparel, we’ve manufactured millions of tops for brands ranging from boutique labels to large-scale retailers. In this guide, we’ll share our expertise on how to source the best tank tops for your collection.

1. Why Every Fashion Collection Needs Quality Tanks

Tank tops provide a low-risk, high-reward entry point for brands. They are relatively simple to manufacture, which keeps the unit cost low, but they are essential for customers year-round. A high-quality basic tank creates brand loyalty; if a customer loves the fit and feel of your basic top, they are far more likely to invest in your more expensive pieces.

Sourcing from China gives you access to an incredible variety of knit fabrics and specialized construction techniques. Whether you need a simple cotton rib or a technical moisture-wicking racerback, the Chinese textile ecosystem offers the best combination of quality and value. At Shanlinyang, we help you leverage this ecosystem to build a collection that meets your target price point without sacrificing quality.

2. Popular Tank Top Silhouettes and Styles

Variety is key to capturing different market segments. Our factory can produce a wide range of silhouettes to suit your brand’s aesthetic.

Basic Ribbed Tanks

A classic staple, often made from cotton/spandex blends. We ensure the ribbing is consistent and the neckline is reinforced to prevent stretching after wear.

Racerback and Y-Back Styles

Popular in both activewear and casual collections, racerbacks offer freedom of movement and a sporty look. We pay close attention to the armhole construction to ensure a comfortable fit that doesn't gap.

Longline and Cropped Tanks

Trend-driven silhouettes like longline tanks (offering more coverage) and cropped tanks (perfect for high-waisted bottoms) are in high demand. We provide precise measurement charts to ensure these hits perfectly on the body.

Built-in Bra Tanks

For added functionality, we can manufacture tops with integrated shelf bras. This requires specialized expertise in handling different fabric weights and elastic tensions to provide support without discomfort.

3. Choosing the Right Fabric for Your Tops

The "feel" of the fabric is what your customers will notice first. Here are the most popular options we offer:

Combed Cotton and Supima Cotton

For high-end basics, combed cotton provides a smooth, soft finish that feels luxurious against the skin. Supima cotton is even finer and more durable, ideal for premium brands.

Modal and Viscose

These fabrics offer a beautiful silky drape and a cool hand-feel. They are perfect for dressier camisoles and lightweight summer tops.

Performance Synthetics (Nylon/Polyester)

For activewear tanks, we use Nylon or Polyester blends with high spandex content. These are moisture-wicking, quick-drying, and durable enough for intense workouts.

Rib-Knit and Waffle Knit

Adding texture to your tops can elevate a simple design. We offer various rib widths and waffle patterns that add visual interest and a cozy feel to your collection.

4. Customization and Branding for Tank Tops

Make your tops uniquely yours with our extensive private label services.

Print and Embroidery Options

From large graphic prints to small tonal embroidery on the chest, we can apply your brand’s designs using multiple techniques. We ensure that the prints are soft and don't crack when the fabric stretches.

Neckline and Hem Variations

Customize the look with different necklines (scoop, V-neck, high-neck) and hem styles (curved, raw edge, or traditional top-stitch). These small changes can completely transform a basic design.

Labels and Packaging

We provide custom printed neck labels (tagless), woven labels, and branded hangtags. For high-volume basics, we can also provide custom retail packaging, including branded polybags or cardboard sleeves.

5. MOQ, Pricing, and Production Lead Times

Understanding the logistical side of manufacturing helps you plan your inventory effectively.

Low MOQ for Emerging Brands

Our MOQ for custom OEM tank tops is typically 100 pieces per color per style. This allows you to offer a wide variety of colors without carrying excessive stock. For wholesale stock, the minimum is just 2 pieces.

Competitive Pricing

Basic tank tops are one of our most cost-effective categories. FOB prices typically range from US$2.50 to US$6.50 depending on the fabric and order volume. We offer significant discounts for high-volume orders.

Fast Turnaround Times

Sampling for tank tops is fast, usually taking 7-10 days. Once the sample is approved, bulk production typically takes 20-30 days. We offer multiple shipping methods to ensure your products arrive when you need them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the MOQ for custom tank tops?
Our MOQ for custom OEM tank tops is 100 pieces per color. For wholesale stock, you can order from 2 pieces.

Q2: Can you manufacture built-in bra tanks?
Yes, we specialize in technical tops and can manufacture tanks with built-in shelf bras and removable padding.

Q3: What are the best fabrics for gym tanks?
Nylon/Spandex or Polyester/Spandex blends are best for gym wear because they are moisture-wicking and offer great stretch.

Q4: Can I add a custom print to a basic tank?
Absolutely. We offer screen printing, digital printing, and heat transfer options for your logos and designs.

Q5: Do you provide size charts for global markets?
Yes, we have standard size charts for US, EU, and AU markets, or we can manufacture according to your own custom size specifications.
