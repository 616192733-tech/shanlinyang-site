GUIDE — LOUNGEWEAR SOURCING

Lounge Set Manufacturer: Wholesale Coordinated Lounge Wear from China

Loungewear has evolved from basic sleepwear into a fashion category of its own. Coordinated lounge sets are now a staple for every modern boutique and online brand.

The key to a successful loungewear line is the perfect balance of comfort and style. As a lounge set manufacturer based in Xiamen, Shanlinyang Apparel has seen the category explode in popularity. Whether you're looking for cozy knits for winter or breathable linen sets for summer, finding the right manufacturer is the first step. This guide covers the essentials of sourcing wholesale lounge sets from China.

1. The Rise of "Elevated Loungewear"

Today’s consumers are looking for "elevated loungewear"—pieces that are comfortable enough for home but stylish enough for a quick coffee run. This trend, often called "ath-flow," requires higher quality fabrics and more sophisticated silhouettes than traditional pajamas.

When working with a lounge set manufacturer, look for designs that feature wide-leg trousers, oversized sweaters, and coordinated crop tops. These sets are highly versatile and appeal to a wide demographic.

2. Selecting the Best Loungewear Fabrics

Fabric choice is the most important decision you will make for your lounge sets. The feel against the skin is everything. Popular choices include:

  • French Terry: A classic for hoodies and joggers, offering a soft loopback texture.
  • Modal and Bamboo: Known for their incredible softness and eco-friendly properties.
  • Ribbed Knit: Provides a more "dressed-up" look and excellent stretch.
  • Cotton-Spandex Blends: Offer durability and a snug, comfortable fit.

3. Private Label vs. Custom Manufacturing

If you want to launch quickly, choose a manufacturer with a strong "Open Catalog." You can pick from their existing lounge set designs and add your private labels. This is much more cost-effective for small runs. For brands with a specific vision, "Custom OEM" allows you to modify lengths, add unique pockets, or create entirely new shapes using your tech packs.

4. Sizing and Fit Considerations

Loungewear fit is generally more relaxed, but "oversized" must still be "well-fitted." A professional manufacturer will provide a size chart that ensures the drape is correct. For sets, it is vital that the waistband of the bottoms is secure but not restrictive. We recommend using high-quality elastic and adjustable drawstrings for maximum comfort.

5. Quality Control for Soft Fabrics

Soft, stretchy fabrics can be tricky to sew. Issues like "puckering" or "wavy seams" are common in low-quality factories. Ensure your manufacturer uses the correct needle types and stitch lengths for the specific fabric. At Shanlinyang, our QC team performs a "wash test" on all loungewear samples to ensure there is no excessive shrinking or pilling after use.

6. Why Xiamen is the Hub for Premium Loungewear

Xiamen is home to some of China’s most advanced garment factories, specializing in high-end export products. By sourcing from a Xiamen-based lounge set manufacturer like Shanlinyang, you benefit from a skilled workforce and proximity to world-class fabric markets. This ensures your loungewear brand stands out for its quality and craftsmanship.
