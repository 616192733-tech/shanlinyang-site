FAQ

Quick answers for sourcing buyers

Which guide should I read first?

If this is your first order, read the first clothing order guide. If you are comparing suppliers, read the manufacturer selection guide and factory verification guide.

Do these guides support US and European buyers?

Yes. They focus on practical buyer concerns for the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Germany, France and EU markets: sizing, labels, DDP, FBA, 3PL, packing, QC and replenishment planning.

Can Shanlinyang quote from photos or a rough idea?

Yes. You can send reference photos, links, a sketch or a tech pack. For more accurate pricing, include fabric direction, size range, quantity per color, logo/label needs and destination country.
