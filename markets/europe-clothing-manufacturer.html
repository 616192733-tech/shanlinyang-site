EUROPEAN-MARKET REQUIREMENTS

Build compliance into the product brief

EU size grading

Use your approved size chart or review a proposed specification. Fit, measurements and grading are confirmed through samples before bulk cutting.

Textile labelling

Supply final fibre composition, care, origin, responsible-party and language requirements for the destination market. Labels are produced from approved artwork.

Material documentation

State required REACH, colourfastness, fibre, trim or buyer-specific test documentation before sampling. Availability and cost depend on the selected materials and test scope.

DELIVERY ACROSS EUROPE

PROCESS

From technical brief to warehouse

  1. Define the market

    Share destination countries, sizes, quantities, materials, labels and compliance requirements.

  2. Approve sample

    Confirm fit, construction, colour, labels and packaging on a physical sample.

  4. Ship with documents

    Confirm cartons, packing list, commercial invoice and freight terms before dispatch.

EUROPE BUYER FAQ

Common sourcing questions

Can you produce to European sizing?
Yes. We can work from your approved chart or prepare a proposed chart for sample approval before bulk production.
Can you provide compliance documentation?
Available documents depend on the fabric, trims and required test scope. List every destination-market requirement before sampling so availability, timing and cost can be confirmed.
Can you deliver DDP?
DDP may be available for suitable routes and order sizes. The quotation will state exactly which duties, taxes and delivery services are included.
