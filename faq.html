KNOWLEDGE BASE — BUYER FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything buyers ask before a first order — MOQ, customization, production, payment and shipping. Can't find your answer? Ask us directly; we reply within 24 hours.

MOQ & ordering

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Custom OEM/ODM production starts at 100 pieces per color per style. In-stock wholesale styles can be ordered from 2 pieces.
Can I mix sizes within the MOQ?
Yes — the 100 pieces can be split across a size curve such as S–XL or XL–5XL at no extra cost.
Can I order a test batch before custom production?
Yes. Most brands order 2–50 pieces of stock styles first, validate sell-through, then move winners into 100-piece branded runs.

Customization

Can you manufacture from a reference photo only?
Yes. Our ODM service develops patterns from a sketch or photo, including fabric sourcing. A counter sample is produced in 7–12 days for approval.
What branding options are included?
Logo print or embroidery, woven neck labels, printed care labels, hangtags and branded poly bags — all available from the 100-piece MOQ.
Do you protect exclusive designs?
Yes. We sign NDAs on request and do not resell customer-exclusive designs or prints.

Production & quality

How long does production take?
Sampling 7–12 days; bulk production 20–35 days; air express to US/EU adds 5–8 days.
How is quality controlled?
Three checkpoints: incoming fabric inspection, inline sewing QC, and final AQL inspection with a photographed report. Third-party inspection is welcome.
What size standards do you use?
Garments are graded to US and EU size charts; plus-size styles use dedicated curve blocks for XL–5XL.

Payment & shipping

What payment methods do you accept?
T/T bank transfer, Alibaba Trade Assurance, PayPal and Wise. Standard terms: 30% deposit, 70% before shipment.
How do you ship to the US and Europe?
DHL, FedEx and UPS air express for orders under ~300kg; sea freight (LCL/FCL) with door-to-door service for larger volumes.
Who handles customs and duties?
We ship DAP by default (you handle import duties) and can quote DDP door-to-door including duties for US and most EU countries.
