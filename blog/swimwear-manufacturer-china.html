Swimwear Manufacturer in China: Complete Sourcing Guide for Beachwear Brands

PUBLISHED 2026-06-23 · SHANLINYANG APPAREL FACTORY TEAM · ~13 MIN READ

The swimwear market is one of the most vibrant and fast-paced sectors in the fashion industry. For a brand to succeed, its products must survive salt water, chlorine, intense sun, and frequent washing—all while maintaining a perfect, flattering fit. This makes swimwear one of the most technical garments to produce. If you are looking for a swimwear manufacturer in China, you need a partner who understands the unique properties of elasticated textiles and the precision required for specialized beachwear construction.

Based in Xiamen, a hub for high-end textile production, Shanlinyang Apparel specializes in helping international brands create stunning swimwear collections. In this guide, we'll walk you through the essential steps of sourcing swimwear from China, from fabric selection to the final quality audit.

1. The Fabric Foundation: Performance and Protection

In swimwear, the fabric is everything. As a leading swimwear manufacturer in China, we guide our clients through three main considerations when selecting materials:

Nylon/Spandex (Polyamide): This is the most popular choice for bikinis and fashion swimwear. It is incredibly soft, has a beautiful sheen, and provides excellent 4-way stretch. It’s comfortable against the skin and dries relatively quickly.

Polyester/Spandex: More durable than nylon, polyester is resistant to pilling and handles chlorine much better. It is the go-to choice for competitive swimwear and sublimated (printed) designs because it holds color exceptionally well.

Sustainable Options: The modern consumer values the planet. We offer high-quality recycled fabrics like Econyl® (regenerated nylon from fishing nets) and Repreve® (recycled polyester from plastic bottles). These options allow your brand to market "eco-friendly swimwear" without sacrificing performance.

2. Technical Features: Chlorine Resistance and UV Protection

Standard fabrics will deteriorate quickly in a pool or on a beach. When working with a swimwear manufacturer, you must ensure your fabrics are treated for the elements:

  • Chlorine Resistance: We use Spandex (Lycra) fibers that are specifically engineered to resist the damaging effects of chlorine, preventing the garment from losing its elasticity and becoming "baggy."
  • UPF Ratings: For children's swimwear and rash guards, we source fabrics with a UPF 50+ rating, which blocks 98% of the sun's harmful rays.
  • Salt Water Testing: We ensure that all fabrics and trims (like buckles and rings) are tested for corrosion resistance in saline environments.

3. Construction Excellence: Lining and Stitching

The internal construction of a swimsuit is just as important as the external design. A professional swimwear manufacturer in China pays close attention to:

Lining: We recommend a high-quality lining for all swimwear to prevent transparency when wet and to provide a comfortable, supportive feel. We often use a lightweight, color-matched nylon lining or a "power mesh" for tummy control areas.

Seams and Edges: Swimwear requires specialized machines. We use "zigzag" or "three-needle" coverstitching for edges to ensure the seams can stretch along with the fabric without snapping. For a premium look, we also offer "seamless" finishes where the edges are bagged out and the stitching is hidden inside.

4. Customizing Your Beachwear Line

How do you make your brand stand out in a sea of bikinis? It’s in the customization. As a full-service swimwear manufacturer, Shanlinyang offers numerous ways to personalize your collection:

  • Custom Hardware: We can source or create custom-branded metal rings, sliders, and clasps. We ensure these are "salt-spray tested" to prevent tarnishing.
  • Digital and Sublimation Printing: These methods allow for unlimited colors and high-definition patterns. We can print your unique designs directly onto the fabric before cutting.
  • Removable Padding: We can include custom-shaped foam inserts for bras and bikini tops, offering your customers adjustable support.
  • Eco-Friendly Branding: Align your brand with sustainability by using recycled paper hang tags and biodegradable polybags for your packaging.

5. Quality Control: The Water and Stretch Test

Swimwear failures are disastrous for a brand's reputation. At Shanlinyang, our quality control process for swimwear and beachwear is rigorous:

  1. Colorfastness Test: We test fabrics in both chlorinated and salt water to ensure the colors don't run or fade prematurely.
  2. The Stretch and Recovery Check: We ensure the garment returns to its original shape after being stretched to its limit.
  3. Fitting on Models: Because swimwear is so dependent on fit, we use professional fit models to ensure the sizing is accurate and the coverage is appropriate across our entire size range.
  4. Hardware Inspection: We check every clasp and adjuster to ensure they are smooth and function perfectly.

6. MOQs and Lead Times for Swimwear Brands

The swimwear industry is highly seasonal. Timing is everything. Many swimwear manufacturers in China have high MOQs, but Shanlinyang supports growing brands with MOQs starting at 100-200 pieces per style.

Sampling: Takes 10-15 days. We recommend starting your sampling in the autumn (September-October) to have your bulk production ready for the spring launch.

Bulk Production: Typically takes 30-45 days once the samples are approved and fabric is sourced. We offer fast shipping options to the US, Europe, and Australia to ensure you don't miss the summer season.

FAQ: Sourcing Swimwear from China

Can you help me design a custom print?

Yes. If you provide your artwork in a high-resolution format, our team can help you layout the print on the garment patterns to ensure perfect alignment.

What is the difference between Nylon and Polyester swimwear?

Nylon is softer and more 'fashion-focused,' while polyester is more durable and resistant to chlorine, making it better for active swimming and frequent use.

Do you offer plus-size swimwear?

Yes. We have specialized patterns for plus-size swimwear up to 5XL, ensuring a flattering and supportive fit for all body types.

How do I know if the metal parts won't rust?

We use high-grade alloys that are coated with a protective layer. We perform 'salt spray tests' in our lab to simulate long-term exposure to ocean conditions.

What is the best time of year to order swimwear?

To launch in the spring (March/April), we recommend starting your design and sampling phase by October. This allows for a stress-free production cycle.
